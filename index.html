RAW MATERIALS

Precise Ingredients & Chemical Science

🧬

Thymolphthalein (C28H30O4)

pH Indicator • 1.0 g

Colorless in acidic/neutral conditions. Turns intense blue above pH 9.3–10.5. The heart of the disappearing effect.

🧪

Ethyl Alcohol (C2H5OH)

Solvent • 100 mL

Dissolves thymolphthalein powder perfectly. Volatile and helps quick drying.

💧

Distilled Water (H2O)

Diluent • 900 mL

Creates the final solution volume. Must be pure to avoid premature pH interference.

⚠️

Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH)

3 Molar • 10 mL

Raises pH to ~10–11 instantly. Turns the solution deep blue. Handle with care (caustic base).

Step-by-Step Laboratory Process

Follow these exact steps as demonstrated in the official video

01
Dissolve 1 g thymolphthalein in 100 mL ethyl alcohol

Stir until completely clear. Alcohol acts as the primary solvent.

02
Add 900 mL distilled water

Solution may turn milky white — this is normal.

03
Slowly add 10 mL of 3 M NaOH

Solution instantly turns dark blue. pH reaches ~10–11.

04
Store in sealed bottle

Your disappearing ink is now ready to use!

Quality Control

  • pH verified at 10–11
  • Color intensity test on fabric
  • Disappearance time < 15 seconds with breath/vinegar
  • Zero residue after 2 minutes

Storage

Store in an amber glass bottle in a cool, dark place. Shelf life: 12+ months when sealed. Keep away from acids.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the ink last before disappearing?

5–15 seconds naturally (with breath). Instantly with vinegar.

Is it safe for skin/fabric?

Yes when diluted. Always do a patch test. Not for ingestion.

Can I make it at home?

Yes — full recipe is on this site. Adult supervision required for NaOH.
