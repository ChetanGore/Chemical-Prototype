PhantomInk

Disappearing Ink

The ultimate pH-responsive ink that magically turns from deep ocean blue to completely invisible in seconds. This project showcases acid-base chemistry in a dramatic and engaging way.

🪄
BLUE → INVISIBLE

Ingredients & Chemical Science

Thymolphthalein (1 gram)

This is the key pH indicator. It stays colorless in neutral or acidic conditions but turns into a rich, deep blue color when the pH goes above 9.3–10.5. When the pH drops again (due to CO₂ in air or vinegar), the color disappears completely.

Ethyl Alcohol (100 mL)

Acts as the primary solvent. Thymolphthalein powder dissolves very well in alcohol but not easily in water. It also helps the ink dry faster when applied on any surface.

Distilled Water (900 mL)

Used to dilute the solution to create the final working volume of approximately 1 liter. Pure distilled water is important to avoid any minerals that could affect the pH stability.

Sodium Hydroxide 3M (10 mL)

This strong base raises the pH of the solution to around 10–11, which triggers the blue color of thymolphthalein. It must be handled carefully with gloves and eye protection during preparation.

Step-by-Step Preparation Process

01

Dissolve 1 gram of thymolphthalein in 100 mL ethyl alcohol.

Stir continuously until the solution becomes completely clear.

02

Add 900 mL of distilled water slowly while stirring.

The mixture may appear milky white at this stage. This is normal.

03

Carefully add 10 mL of 3M Sodium Hydroxide.

Add drop by drop while stirring. The solution will instantly turn deep blue.

04

Transfer to a sealed container.

Your disappearing ink is now ready for use and demonstration.
