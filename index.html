Ingredients & Chemical Science

Thymolphthalein

1 g

The magic molecule that changes color with pH.

Ethyl Alcohol

100 mL

Perfect solvent for the indicator.

Distilled Water

900 mL

Pure dilution for perfect balance.

3M NaOH

10 mL

Creates the alkaline environment.

Step-by-Step Process

01 • Dissolve 1g thymolphthalein in 100 mL ethyl alcohol
02 • Add 900 mL distilled water
03 • Slowly add 10 mL 3M NaOH
04 • Store in sealed bottle
