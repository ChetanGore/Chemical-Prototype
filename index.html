Ingredients & Chemical Science

Thymolphthalein (1 g)

pH indicator that turns deep blue in alkaline conditions and becomes colorless when pH drops. The key to the disappearing effect.

Ethyl Alcohol (100 mL)

Primary solvent that dissolves thymolphthalein effectively and helps the ink dry quickly.

Distilled Water (900 mL)

Dilutes the solution to final volume while maintaining purity.

Sodium Hydroxide 3M (10 mL)

Raises pH to create the blue color. Handle with care during preparation.

Step-by-Step Process

01

Dissolve 1g thymolphthalein in 100 mL ethyl alcohol

02

Add 900 mL distilled water

03

Slowly add 10 mL 3M NaOH

04

Store in sealed bottle

Quality Control

Tested for pH 10–11, vibrant blue color, and disappearance within 15 seconds using breath or vinegar.

Storage

Store in cool, dark place in amber bottle. Shelf life: 12+ months when sealed.
